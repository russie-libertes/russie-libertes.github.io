__"Corruption in Russia is a major plague and a human rights violation. It is also a trap and a risk factor for French and European companies. In the current context of tensions at the European level, it is important to fight this plague that feeds the system and its abuses.__
__This report answers the following questions: What can companies do to fight corruption in Russia? What can France do? What can European States, institutions and organisations do?"__
