(more info here: [https://amrusrights.org/](https://amrusrights.org/) ) — Washington DC : Friday, April 4, 2014, 12:00 – 1:00 pm, Voesar Conference Room, The George Washington University, 1957 E Street NW, Suite 412 (more info here: [https://www.gwu.edu/~ieresgwu/assets/docs/04.04.14_Prokopiev.pdf](https://www.gwu.edu/%7Eieresgwu/assets/docs/04.04.14_Prokopiev.pdf) )
